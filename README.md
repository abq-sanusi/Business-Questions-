
# Answering Business Quesions with SQL

# Project Overview

For this project, a total of 4 tables were joined together to answer some business questions. But for easy and smooth analysis, all tables were joined together via "left join" to for a big comprehensive table. 

# Business questions

The following questions answered with the comprehensive table are:

- the top rated customers based on total sales, total quantity

- the sales regions that generate top rated customers

- the top selling products based on total sales, total quantity

- the overall best product categories

- the Year on Year growth to measure the increase and decrease in sales profit across different years

- the regional product performance

- highest demanded products

- low performing products

# Conclusion

The perfection of the joining delivered accurate results while answering the business questions.
